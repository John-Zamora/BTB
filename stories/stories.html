FAIR PAY

We believe that the creators of our button-downs deserve the best. We aid them in acquiring TESDA certifications and provide them with the necessary health benefits, on top of a fair wage for their work. We value their labor more than anything else, as they are our dream-makers behind the By the Button vision.

SUSTAINED WITH CARE

The founders behind By the Button are John Zamora and Pauline Baterna, who are also the developers and designers of the website. Both founders advocate for the fair wage of garment workers and sustainable, ethical fashion. With an eye for structure and aesthetic, they are also the visionaries of the By the Button vision.
